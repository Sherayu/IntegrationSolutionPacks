# Insurance Domain Process Flows — ACORD Capability Model Reference

> Generated on 2026-07-09 | Domain: Insurance | Framework: ACORD (Association for Cooperative Operations Research and Development)


## ACORD Business Capability Model Reference

The ACORD (Association for Cooperative Operations Research and Development) Capability Model defines the standard business capabilities for insurance institutions. The table below lists all relevant capabilities across 10 Level-1 domains. Each process flow in this document is mapped to its relevant ACORD capability codes.

### Product

| Capability | Code | Description |
|-----------|------|-------------|
| **Product Lifecycle Management** | `IN001` | Design, develop, and manage insurance products across lifecycle from concept to retirement |
| **Product Configuration & Rating** | `IN002` | Configure product rules, rating algorithms, and coverage options |
| **Product Filing & Compliance** | `IN003` | Manage regulatory product filings, approvals, and compliance across jurisdictions |
| **Product Performance Analytics** | `IN004` | Monitor product profitability, loss ratios, and distribution performance |

### Distribution

| Capability | Code | Description |
|-----------|------|-------------|
| **Channel Management** | `IN005` | Manage distribution channels including agents, brokers, bancassurance, and direct |
| **Producer/Agent Lifecycle Management** | `IN006` | Onboard, license, appoint, and manage producers and agents |
| **Commission Management** | `IN007` | Calculate, process, and disburse commissions across compensation structures |
| **Lead & Campaign Management** | `IN008` | Manage marketing campaigns, lead generation, and distribution analytics |

### Underwriting

| Capability | Code | Description |
|-----------|------|-------------|
| **New Business Submission** | `IN009` | Receive, validate, and triage new business submissions from producers |
| **Risk Assessment & Scoring** | `IN010` | Assess risk using rules, models, and third-party data sources |
| **Underwriting Decision** | `IN011` | Make underwriting decisions with authority-based approval and referral workflows |
| **Quote Generation & Bind** | `IN012` | Generate quotes, bind coverage, and issue evidence of insurance |

### Policy

| Capability | Code | Description |
|-----------|------|-------------|
| **Policy Issuance & Administration** | `IN013` | Issue policies, manage policy documents, and maintain in-force records |
| **Policy Servicing & Adjustments** | `IN014` | Process mid-term adjustments, endorsements, and policy changes |
| **Renewal & Termination** | `IN015` | Manage renewal lifecycle, non-renewal notices, cancellations, and reinstatements |

### Claims

| Capability | Code | Description |
|-----------|------|-------------|
| **First Notice of Loss** | `IN016` | Capture claim notifications through multi-channel intake and triage |
| **Claims Investigation & Evaluation** | `IN017` | Investigate claims, gather evidence, assess liability, and estimate reserves |
| **Claims Adjudication & Payment** | `IN018` | Adjudicate claims, approve payments, and manage benefit disbursement |
| **Subrogation & Recovery** | `IN019` | Pursue subrogation, salvage, and third-party recoveries |

### Reinsurance

| Capability | Code | Description |
|-----------|------|-------------|
| **Treaty Management** | `IN020` | Manage proportional and non-proportional treaty agreements and session schedules |
| **Facultative Placement** | `IN021` | Place facultative reinsurance for individual large or complex risks |
| **Reinsurance Accounting & Bordereaux** | `IN022` | Prepare premium and loss bordereaux, manage cessions, and reconcile recoveries |

### Financial & Actuarial

| Capability | Code | Description |
|-----------|------|-------------|
| **Actuarial Modeling & Pricing** | `IN023` | Develop pricing models, rate indications, and actuarial assumptions |
| **Reserve Estimation & Management** | `IN024` | Estimate loss reserves, IBNR, and actuarial liabilities |
| **Catastrophe Modeling** | `IN025` | Model natural and man-made catastrophe exposure and accumulation risk |

### Customer

| Capability | Code | Description |
|-----------|------|-------------|
| **Customer Onboarding & KYC** | `IN026` | Onboard customers with identity verification, AML screening, and due diligence |
| **Customer Relationship Management** | `IN027` | Manage customer interactions, loyalty programs, and cross-sell opportunities |

### Risk & Compliance

| Capability | Code | Description |
|-----------|------|-------------|
| **Enterprise Risk Management** | `IN028` | Manage ERM framework, risk appetite, capital adequacy, and ORSA processes |
| **Regulatory Compliance** | `IN029` | Ensure compliance with Solvency II, NAIC, APRA, and other regulatory regimes |
| **Internal Audit & Assurance** | `IN030` | Conduct internal audits, control testing, and assurance activities |

### Enterprise & IT

| Capability | Code | Description |
|-----------|------|-------------|

### 1.1 Product Concept & Feasibility

**Description:** Product manager submits new product concept. Feasibility assessment evaluates market demand, regulatory landscape, and profitability targets.

**Actors:** Product Manager, Actuary, Compliance Officer  
**Systems:** Idea Portal, Product Lifecycle Mgmt (Duck Creek), Market Intelligence (Mintel), CRM (Salesforce Financial Services)

#### Sequence Diagram

```mermaid
sequenceDiagram
    participant PM as Product Manager
    participant IP as Idea Portal
    participant PLM as Product Lifecycle<br/>(Duck Creek)
    participant MI as Market Intelligence<br/>(Mintel)
    participant ACT as Actuarial System<br/>(SAS)
    participant CRM as CRM<br/>(Salesforce Financial Svcs)
    PM->>IP: Submit product concept<br/>name=Pay-As-You-Drive Motor, LoB=Motor
    IP->>PLM: Register concept<br/>status=Feasibility Assessment
    PLM->>MI: Request market analysis<br/>segment=Young Drivers, region=NSW
    MI-->>PLM: Market data<br/>TAM=250M, growth=8% YoY
    PLM->>ACT: Request profitability model<br/>premium range=800-1,500, LER=55%
    ACT-->>PLM: Profitability estimate<br/>NPV=12.5M, IRR=18%, payback=2yr
    PLM->>CRM: Check distribution fit<br/>existing broker network coverage
    CRM-->>PLM: Distribution assessment<br/>65% broker coverage in target segment
    PLM->>PLM: Score feasibility<br/>market_viability=High, regulatory=Medium
    PLM-->>PM: Feasibility report<br/>score=78/100, recommend=Proceed
```

**Description:** Product team prepares rate and form filing submission for regulatory approval. Filing package includes actuarial justification, proposed rates, and policy forms.

**Actors:** Product Manager, Actuary, Compliance Officer, Regulator  
**Systems:** Product Lifecycle Mgmt (Duck Creek), Actuarial System (SAS), Filing Portal (Regulator), Document Mgmt (M-Files)

#### Sequence Diagram

```mermaid
sequenceDiagram
    participant PM as Product Manager
    participant PLM as Product Lifecycle<br/>(Duck Creek)
    participant ACT as Actuarial System<br/>(SAS)
    participant REG as Regulator Portal<br/>(APRA / ASIC)
    participant DOC as Document Mgmt<br/>(M-Files)
    PM->>PLM: Initiate rate filing<br/>product=PAYD-MTR-001, state=NSW
    PLM->>ACT: Generate rate indication<br/>proposed rates vs current, impact analysis
    ACT-->>PLM: Rate indication<br/>+8.5% avg, LER 55.2%, expense ratio 32%
    PLM->>DOC: Compile filing package<br/>filing_memo, actuarial memo, policy forms
    DOC-->>PLM: Filing package ready<br/>status=Compiled, 12 documents
    PLM->>REG: Submit rate & form filing<br/>portal submission, GL-2026-042
    REG-->>PLM: Filing acknowledged<br/>filing_id=GL-2026-042, status=Under Review
```
